STYLE DIRECTION (INSPIRED BY JASPER.AI)

Design the website with a visual style inspired by the Jasper.ai marketing site, while remaining original.

The design should feel AI-native, modern, and high-energy, similar to the aesthetic used on jasper.ai where bold typography, vibrant gradients, and clean product visuals communicate a premium AI platform.

Use the following style characteristics:

Typography

Use Plus Jakarta Sans across the entire site.

Headings:
Plus Jakarta Sans Bold

Body copy:
Plus Jakarta Sans Regular

Headlines should be large, bold, and highly readable with strong contrast against the background. Jasper’s design relies heavily on bold headline typography to drive visual hierarchy and clarity.

Color System

Use a vibrant AI gradient palette similar to the style used on Jasper’s brand identity.

Gradient accents should include combinations of:

Electric violet
Hot pink
Deep purple
Electric blue

These colors should appear in gradients behind sections, UI highlights, and hero elements. Jasper’s brand identity prominently features energetic colors such as pink and violet to create a distinctive AI aesthetic.

Backgrounds should remain mostly white or very light neutral so the gradients feel premium rather than overwhelming.

UI Style

Follow a modern AI SaaS visual language:

• rounded card components
• subtle shadows
• glass or layered panels
• soft glow effects around AI elements
• floating UI elements in hero visuals
• clean grid alignment

Avoid heavy borders. Use light elevation and shadows instead.

Illustration and Imagery

Avoid stock illustrations.

Use product UI visuals and interface mockups as the primary imagery.

Hero and feature sections should showcase realistic software interfaces and AI workflows.

The site should feel product-led rather than illustration-led.

Section Background Treatments

Introduce gradient lighting behind some sections to mimic Jasper’s visual rhythm.

Examples:

• soft radial gradient behind the hero
• subtle gradient glow behind the AI engine diagram
• light gradient transition between sections

These gradients should be subtle and elegant, not overpowering.

Overall Feel

The final design should feel like a premium AI SaaS company similar in visual confidence to Jasper, Linear, or Stripe.

Key traits:

Bold headline typography
Clean layout
Bright AI gradients
Minimal but polished UI components
Strong product visuals

The result should feel like a top-tier AI platform marketing site built for modern SaaS companies.
