STYLE DIRECTION — BASED ON JASPER.AI

Design the website with a visual style closely inspired by the Jasper.ai marketing site aesthetic, focusing on human-centered imagery, bold typography, and distinctive brand patterns rather than gradients.

The overall feeling should be:

Modern marketing platform
Human-centric AI brand
Editorial, confident, and clean
Not futuristic, not overly “techy”

The design should feel closer to a modern marketing brand like Jasper, Notion, or Webflow rather than a typical AI startup.

IMAGERY STYLE

Use real people photography as the primary imagery throughout the site.

Images should feature:

• diverse professionals
• marketers collaborating
• teams working with laptops
• people presenting ideas
• casual but professional work environments

Photography style:

• natural lighting
• confident expressions
• warm but professional tone
• slightly stylized backgrounds

Avoid:

3D illustrations
AI robots
generic tech illustrations
floating gradients

Human images should be integrated with graphic patterns or shapes.

The goal is to make the AI product feel human-driven and approachable.

PATTERN SYSTEM

Use brand patterns as a core visual element, similar to Jasper’s use of dots, grids, and shapes.

Patterns should appear in:

hero backgrounds
section transitions
image frames
graphic accents

Pattern styles to use:

• dotted grids
• repeating geometric shapes
• subtle checker or matrix grids
• simple abstract pattern overlays

Patterns should be subtle but recognizable as part of the brand identity.

They should appear behind photography and UI cards.

BACKGROUND TREATMENT

Do NOT rely on gradients as the primary visual style.

Instead use:

• white backgrounds
• light neutral backgrounds
• subtle patterned sections
• occasional soft color blocks

Sections should alternate between:

white
very light neutral
pattern overlay sections

This creates visual rhythm across the page.

TYPOGRAPHY

Use Plus Jakarta Sans.

Headings:
Plus Jakarta Sans Bold

Body copy:
Plus Jakarta Sans Regular

Typography should be very bold and editorial.

Headline scale should be large.

Hero headline: ~64px
Section headline: ~40px
Body text: ~16px

Headlines should be short and punchy.

Avoid long paragraphs.

LAYOUT STYLE

Layout should feel editorial and spacious.

Use large section spacing and clear vertical rhythm.

Section spacing: ~120px

Cards should feel minimal.

Rounded corners: ~12px

Subtle shadows.

Avoid heavy borders.

GRAPHIC ACCENTS

Add small brand graphic elements such as:

• abstract shapes
• color blocks
• patterned overlays
• brand stickers or labels

These should appear around images or sections.

The goal is to give the site visual personality, not just UI components.

PRODUCT VISUALS

Product UI should appear in sections that explain the platform.

UI mockups should look like real SaaS dashboards.

Avoid overly complex UI illustrations.

Keep UI visuals clean and believable.

VISUAL RHYTHM

The page should alternate between:

human photography sections

product UI sections

pattern-based graphic sections

This rhythm is important to prevent the site from feeling like a generic SaaS template.

OVERALL BRAND FEEL

The final design should feel:

confident
modern
human
marketing-focused
approachable

The website should look like it belongs to a serious marketing AI platform used by real teams, not a futuristic AI experiment.
