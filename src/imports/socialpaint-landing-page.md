Design a high-end SaaS product marketing landing page for a startup called Socialpaint.

Socialpaint is an AI platform that learns how a company’s design team builds visuals and then generates perfectly on-brand content for the entire company.

The platform integrates with design tools like Figma and Canva, analyzes the micro-decisions designers make (spacing, padding, typography hierarchy, color usage, layout structure, and component usage), and builds a living AI model of the brand called Style DNA.

Once Style DNA is created, anyone in the company can generate brand-consistent graphics instantly without waiting on the design team.

The product sits at the intersection of:

AI content generation
design systems
brand governance

The page must communicate two key outcomes:

• brand consistency across all teams
• dramatically faster content creation

The website should feel comparable in quality to product marketing pages from Stripe, Linear, Figma, Vercel, or Notion.

Avoid generic startup visuals.

This should look like a credible AI SaaS product ready to launch.
